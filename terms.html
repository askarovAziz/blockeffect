Booking Terms

Clear treatment and guest conditions

These terms explain how bookings are confirmed, how pricing is communicated, and what standards apply to all guests and services at Effect Spa.

Professional services only

Effect Spa provides legitimate spa, massage, hammam, and relaxation services only. Any inappropriate request may result in immediate refusal of service.

Booking confirmation

Appointments are confirmed directly by our team. Service type, duration, and applicable price are clarified before the booking is finalized.

Pricing

Promotions and package availability may vary. The final applicable rate is confirmed with the guest before the appointment is accepted.

Cancellations

Please notify us as early as possible if you need to reschedule or cancel. Repeated late cancellations or missed appointments may affect future bookings.

Health information

Guests should share any relevant comfort preferences, allergies, pregnancy, injuries, or recent procedures that may affect service selection or session comfort.

Respectful conduct

We maintain a respectful environment for guests and therapists. Disruptive or inappropriate conduct is not accepted.
