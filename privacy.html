Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect guest information

Data Transparency

Your information is used only for legitimate business communication

Effect Spa uses guest information only to respond to enquiries, arrange bookings, confirm appointments, and provide essential service updates related to our spa services.

Information we collect

Name, phone number, email address, preferred booking details, and any service notes or preferences you voluntarily share with us.

How we use information

To answer questions, confirm reservations, coordinate treatment details, and provide guest support before and after an appointment.

Communication channels

We may communicate through phone, WhatsApp, or email when you contact us or request a booking.

Data sharing

We do not sell guest information. We share data only when reasonably required to operate the business, comply with legal obligations, or protect guests and staff.

Retention

We keep booking and enquiry information only as long as needed for service delivery, guest support, record keeping, and legal compliance.

Your requests

You may contact us at info@effectspa.ae to request updates or deletion of personal information where applicable under local law.
